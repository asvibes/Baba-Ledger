    About Baba's Ledger

    An engineering document intelligence platform, not a generic PDF summarizer.

    Baba's Ledger reads the business and engineering documents that drive construction and procurement decisions — invoices, tenders, contracts, work orders, purchase orders, bills of quantities, delivery challans, and technical specifications — and surfaces what actually matters: amounts, deadlines, penalty clauses, and material specifications.

    Every document is processed locally and deleted after you download your results. Nothing is stored permanently.
